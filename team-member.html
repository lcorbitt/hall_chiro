the heart of our clinic

Since founding Hall Chiropractic & Wellness Center in 1997, Dr. Hall has been an active member in the Overland Park community. He currently serves as the Vice Chair of the Board of Directors for the San Diego Council of Clinics, a board member of the North San Diego Economic Council, San Diegans for Healthcare Coverage, the Blue Shield/UCSF Clinical Leadership Institute, The San Diego Regional Climate Education Partnership, CSUSM University Council and the Palomar College President’s Circle.

Dr. Hall has experience in executive management, strategic program planning, finance, and operational management in both the public and private sector. Under his direction, our clinic has become an organization that serves more than 10,000 individuals annually only at San Diego, employs a staff of more than 60 qualified specialists and has an annual budget of over $15 million.
